Video Game Market Analysis

Python + EDA + Statistical analysis | Pandas · NumPy · Matplotlib · SciPy

Business question: which platforms and genres should be prioritized for 2017 based on historical sales and platform lifecycle patterns.

Method: cleaned and normalized sales data across platforms. Estimated average platform lifespan at 8 years using release and decline patterns. Evaluated regional revenue distribution and compared user scores across platforms through statistical testing.

Result: sales are concentrated in a limited number of platforms. PS4 and XOne show sustained positive trends in the most recent period, supporting prioritization for 2017 planning.

Telecom Plan Revenue Analysis

Python + Metrics modeling + Hypothesis testing | Pandas · NumPy · SciPy · Matplotlib

Business question: which prepaid plan generates higher average monthly revenue and how should advertising budget be allocated between Surf and Ultimate.

Method: constructed monthly revenue metrics per user from raw usage records including calls, messages and mobile data. Applied plan pricing rules and overage charges. Compared average revenue between plans through statistical testing.

Result: average monthly revenue per user is higher under the Ultimate plan than under the Surf plan. A statistical test at a significance level of 0.05 confirms that the difference in average revenue between the two plans is significant. This supports allocating a larger share of advertising budget to the Ultimate plan.

Weather Impact on Chicago Taxi Trips

Python + SQL + EDA + hypothesis testing | Pandas · SciPy · Seaborn · Matplotlib

Business question: how concentrated is taxi demand across neighborhoods and companies in Chicago, and does rain affect travel time between the Loop and O Hare International Airport.

Method: standardized company and neighborhood names and computed demand concentration metrics including mean trip volume, median trip volume, and company market share. Trip durations between the Loop and O Hare were separated by weather conditions and compared through statistical testing at a significance level of 0.05.

Result: taxi demand is highly concentrated. The ten largest companies account for seventy two percent of all recorded trips. Travel time between the Loop and O Hare is longer during rainy conditions and the difference in average duration is statistically significant at the 0.05 significance level.

Instacart Purchase Behavior Analysis

Python + EDA + behavioral aggregation | Pandas · NumPy · Matplotlib

Business question: how frequently do customers reorder products and how concentrated is demand across the product catalog.

Method: processed 478967 order records, removed duplicate entries, standardized categorical variables, and computed basket size, reorder frequency, and product level demand concentration metrics.

Result: demand is strongly concentrated in a small subset of products. The average reorder rate across the catalog is thirty nine percent. Purchase activity also follows consistent weekly ordering cycles.
