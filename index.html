About me

Data Scientist

I hold a master’s degree in Astrophysics and a background in Applied Mathematics, where analysis relies on measurement, uncertainty, and verification. I am currently completing the Data Science Bootcamp at TripleTen to formalize and extend this quantitative background toward applied data analysis in operational and business contexts.

I work with Python and SQL to organize data, build metrics, and apply statistical analysis to evaluate performance and support decisions. In the MEGASTAR project, I developed automation tools that reduced scientific data processing time by 95%.

I focus on clear reasoning, transparent assumptions, and reproducible workflows. I value collaboration, clear communication, and solutions that remain maintainable over time.
